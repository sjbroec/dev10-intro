restaurant description

Jeffy D's was founded in 2010 in Robbinsdale, Minnesota. Tired of the average pancake house and fast food options, Jeff Dean introduced a fast-casual breakfast option to the market while maintaining a small-town diner feel. His concept brought together a passion for food and a . Using a familiar layout, Jeff was able to create a healthy, customizable way to eat breakfast all day long.

Now more than 500 restaurants strong, Jeffy D's is thriving. Each restaurant features more than 2000 customizable flavor combinations and allows you to choose your own breakfast adventure.
