Restaurant Description

Jeffy D's was founded in 2010 in Robbinsdale, Minnesota. Tired of the average pancake house and fast food options, Jeff Palacio introduced fast-casual breakfast option to the market while maintaining that diner-feel. His concept brought together his favorite meal of the day and his favorite way to eat. Using a familiar layout, he was able to create a healthy, customizable way to eat breakfast all day long!

Now more than 500 restaurants strong, Jeffy D's is thriving. Each restaurant features more than 2000 customizable flavor combinations and allows you to choose your own breakfast adventure.
